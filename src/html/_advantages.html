Technical advantages

State Pix offers a new approach to real estate investing by making the process more accessible, secure and efficient.

  • Availability of information

    All information about the token and the property is open and transparent.

  • Protection of property rights

    Your rights as the owner are reliably protected thanks to smart contracts and terms of the offer.

  • Simplifying liquidity

    Facilitating the process of buying and selling through p2p transactions.

  • Cost reduction

    Minimizing commissions thanks to off-chain transactions.

  • Secure Storage

    Custodial wallet to securely store your digital assets.

  • Ease of management

    An interface that simplifies operations with objects as much as possible
