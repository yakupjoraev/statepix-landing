State Pix Asset Management Platform

State Pix provides a platform to manage your tokenized real estate. We provide direct access to buying and selling through secure smart contracts. In our ecosystem, every property linked to an NFT is available for purchase directly from the developer or through p2p transactions with other users.

Off-chain transactions for transactions within the ecosystem

StatePix uses off-chain transactions to speed up processes and reduce transaction costs. This allows users to conduct transactions directly on the platform without having to record each transaction on the blockchain.
