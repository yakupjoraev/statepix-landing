Integrating physical and digital ownership

Our technology concept transforms the rights to a digital asset in the form of an NFT into rights to own, manage and profit from a physical object. The token is tied to an object or share of real estate, storing all relevant information about its characteristics, from location and square footage to images and room layout. Intellectual property laws and management company guarantees protect the rights of token holders. Blockchain and off-chain transactions provide instant and secure transactions of tokenized assets.

  • Instant purchase for cryptocurrency

  • Confidentiality possessions

  • Convenient control investments

  • Fractions of one foot up to the whole building

State Pix Asset Management Platform

State Pix provides a platform to manage your tokenized real estate. We provide direct access to buying and selling through secure smart contracts. In our ecosystem, every property linked to an NFT is available for purchase directly from the developer or through p2p transactions with other users.

Off-chain transactions for transactions within the ecosystem

StatePix uses off-chain transactions to speed up processes and reduce transaction costs. This allows users to conduct transactions directly on the platform without having to record each transaction on the blockchain.
